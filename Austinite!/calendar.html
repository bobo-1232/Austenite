Things To Do

Here is a list of things to do, they are open all year long, except for certain holidays. Mark your calendar now.

LBJ Presidential Library

  • Address: 2313 Red River St., Austin, TX 78705
  • Telephone: (512) 721-0200
  • Description: To experience the decades in which President Johnson made decisions that affected the United States politically, educationally, socially, and environmentally, visitors can visit the LBJ Presidential Library to learn more about our 36th president and his decisions.
  • Fun fact: anyone with the first, middle, or last name “Lyndon” gets free admission.

The Escape Game Austin

  • Address: 405 Red River St., Austin, TX 78701
  • Telephone: (512) 643-0043
  • Description: The escape game offers different themes, such as treasure hunting or escaping from a prison. You can bring some friends and collaborate with them to solve puzzles and find clues. Now, you can go to their website and book appointments before your arrival.
  • Fun fact: The site has an overwhelmingly positive rating on Yelp.
